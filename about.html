Dexter Jay Berenio, CPA

Founder | DJB CPA Accounting & Consultancy Services

Dexter Jay Berenio is a Certified Public Accountant with a strong passion for helping small businesses thrive. As the founder of DJB CPA Accounting & Consultancy Services, Dexter brings a people-first approach to accounting—focusing not just on numbers, but on the growth and long-term success of every client.

With hands-on experience in Australian accounting practices, Dexter has developed a keen eye for practical, efficient, and tech-driven solutions. His exposure to client-centric systems and streamlined processes abroad has inspired his mission to bring the best and most attainable of these practices to the Philippine business landscape.

Dexter is committed to making professional accounting accessible, insightful, and transformative—especially for small business owners who want to innovate their operations and achieve greater financial clarity. His vision is simple: to help elevate businesses by simplifying processes and maximizing tax efficiency, every step of the way.
